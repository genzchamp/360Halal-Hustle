Quick Halal Checklist

  • Avoid margin/overnight interest (use swap-free accounts or close before rollover).
  • Trade with knowledge — avoid speculative gambling behaviour.
  • Use strict risk management and keep a trade journal.
  • Be transparent in teaching; avoid guarantees or misleading claims.
